# Stage 4: Delivery to Finance Transition

## Purpose

Ensure that the financial outcome of the engagement is fully and accurately reflected in the organisation's accounts. It is a handover, not an afterthought. It covers revenue recognition, invoice finalisation, and any retention or warranty period obligations.

---

## Entry Criteria (Gate 3 passed)

- The Closure Acknowledgement has been received from the client
- The internal retrospective is complete and a Delivery Knowledge Base entry has been filed
- All practitioners have confirmed next allocations
- All timesheets have been submitted and approved
- The final invoice has been raised or is scheduled with a confirmed date
- The Finance Partner has confirmed the final billing position and any retention schedule

---

## Roles

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Delivery Owner | Confirms all delivery obligations are complete and hands over formally to Finance |
| Finance Partner | Confirms revenue recognition, raises final invoices, and manages the retention schedule |
| Account Executive | Informed of closure for CRM update and relationship planning |

---

## Key Activities

**Timesheet reconciliation.** All time logged against the engagement is reviewed against the delivery plan. Variances are explained and used to calibrate future estimates, not to attribute blame.

**Revenue recognition review.** The Finance Partner confirms that revenue has been, or will be, recognised in the correct periods and amounts. Any adjustments required are documented and processed.

**Retention management.** Where a retention amount is held by the client, the conditions for release are recorded, the release date is diarised, and the Delivery Owner or Account Executive is confirmed as responsible for follow-up at the agreed date.

**CRM and knowledge base update.** The Account Executive updates the CRM with the engagement outcome, key relationships, and any identified opportunities. The Delivery Owner confirms the internal knowledge base entry is complete and accessible.

---

## Metrics

| Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Days from Gate 3 to final invoice paid | Within agreed payment terms | Per engagement | Finance Partner |
| Retention release rate | 100% within agreed timeframe | Per engagement | Finance Partner |
| Timesheet submission compliance at close | 100% | Per engagement | Resource Manager |
| Forecast-to-actual revenue variance by engagement | — | Per engagement | Finance Partner |
| % of engagements with completed Finance Handover Record | 100% | Per engagement | Finance Partner |

---

## Exit Criteria

All of the following must be confirmed before the engagement is marked as closed:

- The Finance Handover Record has been completed and signed off by the Finance Partner
- Revenue has been recognised or scheduled for recognition in the correct periods
- The final invoice has been paid or is tracking within agreed payment terms
- Any retention amounts and release conditions are recorded and diarised
- The CRM record for the engagement has been updated by the Account Executive
- The Delivery Knowledge Base entry has been confirmed as complete by the Delivery Owner
